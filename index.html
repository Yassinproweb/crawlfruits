About Us

We are a grocery store that connects you to fruits & vegetables farmers all around East Africa. We help deliver high quality fresh farm produce in time all seasons all year-long.

We are trusted by over thirty thousand (30k) farmers and we have delivered quality to over forty five million (45M) people and stores in East Africa.

We have won over 13 Gold Medals and have been honored 3 Times to supply fruits and vegetables during the East African Community Presidential Assembly.
